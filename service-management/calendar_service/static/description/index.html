Generate Recurrent Services!

Create rules to generate weekly services

Create rules for every customer you offer your services. Assign specific employees (resources) that do work to complete service. If you accidently assign same resource with same time for another rule, system will give throw a message showing which rule have already taken that resource!

Choose weekday and time that every week such services and its works need to be completed. You can also choose to generate such events every second week if needed.

Integrated with Sales module!

Create sales quotations from provided services? You can do that!

If you assign product (with service type) for your provided service and after providing and completing it, system will automatically create relevant sales quotation for that service.

Such sale quotation will also be related with relevant service you provided to your customer. So you can easily track for which service (and when) any sale order was created.

Fully Automatic

Services are managed automatically

Recurrent events are generated automatically every week, extending planned services for one or any weeks you need. This way you can see planned services in a future.

All services close automatically. When specific service end time passes system marks it as completed. Such behavior also lets automate other features like creating sales quotations.
